About Us

Hess Nonprofit Organization is a dedicated humanitarian organization focused on providing essential aid to refugees across Africa. We allocate 100% of all donations to support our mission of providing food, clean water, and education to those in dire need.

Our Mission: Empowering Refugees, Transforming Lives

Hess Nonprofit’s core mission is to alleviate the suffering of refugees in Africa. We are committed to providing basic necessities such as food, safe drinking water, and educational opportunities to help rebuild the lives of displaced individuals and families.

  • Providing sustainable food and clean water solutions to refugee communities.
  • Supporting educational programs to empower refugee children and adults.
  • Offering immediate relief and long-term solutions to improve living conditions.

Our Impact

These are just a few of the many ways we have made a difference in the lives of refugees in Africa. Every donation we receive helps us continue to support those in need, and we are committed to making the most of every dollar.

Academic Impact

We’ve established educational programs for refugee children and adults, providing them with the skills and knowledge needed to rebuild their futures.

Water Wells Drilling

We’ve drilled numerous wells to provide refugees with access to safe, clean drinking water, improving their overall health and well-being.

Number of Beneficiaries

Through our ongoing projects, we’ve been able to reach thousands of refugees, providing critical support and services in their time of need.

Peace Keeping

We support peacekeeping efforts, ensuring safety and stability in refugee camps and surrounding areas, allowing displaced people to rebuild their lives in peace.

Solar Powered Water Supply

In regions with limited access to electricity, we’ve implemented solar-powered water supply systems, ensuring consistent access to clean water for refugees.

Civic Education

Our civic education programs empower refugees to understand their rights and responsibilities, helping them integrate into new communities and advocate for their needs.

Awards

Through your generous contributions, we have been recognized, and our humanitarian work has been honored with the awards listed below.

Burning Sphere Award - Kenya

In 2020, the then president of Kenya recognized our work during the COVID-19 pandemic. We ensured that those living in refugee camps also received food, sanitizers, water, and clothing during these challenging times.

Peace Keeper Award - South Sudan

In 2022, we received a generous donation of $5,000 from Harry Cooper, the largest donation from an individual. Harry, a U.S. tourist who visited South Sudan during the unrest among communities clashing over water sources for their animals, requested our help to find a lasting solution. We drilled two wells, resolving the issue permanently.

Best Upcoming Charitable NGO - Somaliland

Our small but impactful work in Somaliland was recognized. Amid ongoing insecurity in the region, we partnered with local schools to provide affordable basic education to students who might have otherwise turned to crime. Criminal groups often exploit illiterate youth, brainwashing them into joining their ranks.

NGO Humanitarian Award - Ethiopia

With funds primarily received from individual donors via PayPal (both one-time and monthly contributions), we were able to establish a foothold in Ethiopian refugee camps. Our primary focus has been feeding programs for families facing difficult conditions. Alongside other charitable organizations, we have been running the operation for almost two years now. We hope to see peace return to Ethiopia soon.
